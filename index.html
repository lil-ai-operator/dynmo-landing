FAQ

Questions smart buyers ask.

Is this a real AI assessment or a fake quiz?

It is a deterministic adaptive funnel. The branches change based on your answers, then the final plan is generated client-side from structured signals. That keeps it fast, reliable, and honest.

Does Dynmo save my data anywhere yet?

No backend is wired here yet. Your lead details and plan stay in browser state and local storage on this device. You can copy the plan or use the mail link to send it.

Can you guarantee bookings or revenue?

No. Dynmo helps remove friction, improve response speed, and create stronger systems. Results depend on your offer, traffic, follow-through, and market.

What if I do not even have a business yet?

The funnel still works. You get a starter path first, then a likely first Dynmo pack once you have an idea and channel worth pushing.
