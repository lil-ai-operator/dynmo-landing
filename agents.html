Ways Dynmo Can Help

One clear problem. One custom AI helper.

Dynmo keeps the explanation simple: booking help, follow-up help, admin help, or a first-move idea helper.

Booking Helper

For missed calls, slow replies, and booking leaks.

Useful when hot leads message, ring, or fill a form and nobody gets back fast enough.

  • Instant first replies
  • Missed-call rescue
  • Clear next-step prompts
Follow-Up Helper

For leads that go cold and customers who never come back.

Useful when you know demand exists, but the follow-up is too slow or too inconsistent.

  • Quote chasing
  • Repeat booking nudges
  • Review and referral prompts
Admin Helper

For inbox overload, messy handoffs, and too much manual admin.

Useful when leads, tasks, and updates land in too many places and nobody owns the next step.

  • Lead routing
  • Simple triage
  • Calmer team handoff
Idea Helper

For people who need a clearer first move.

Useful when you want something built but need the offer, audience, or first channel to become clearer first.

  • Offer shaping
  • Audience clarity
  • First-step action plan
Which One Fits?

Choose by bottleneck, not by jargon.

Missed enquiries: Booking Helper

Slow follow-up: Follow-Up Helper

Too much admin: Admin Helper

Not sure where to start: Idea Helper
